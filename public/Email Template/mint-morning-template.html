Rand strengthens as dollar weakens on Fed rate hold

The South African rand firmed against a weaker dollar on Thursday after the US Federal Reserve held interest rates steady, easing pressure on emerging market currencies and boosting appetite for risk assets.

The rand traded at R18.42 per dollar at 07:30 SAST, compared with R18.71 at Wednesday's close — a gain of around 1.6%.
