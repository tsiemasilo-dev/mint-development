Suggestions to take it to the next level:

1. Transaction History & Activity

Your Activity page exists but could show more detail — real fintech apps show every buy, sell, dividend, and fee with timestamps, and let users filter/search their history.
2. Recurring/Automatic Investments

Apps like EasyEquities and Acorns let users set up weekly or monthly auto-investments into strategies. This is a big engagement feature — "invest R500 every month into SA Balanced Core."
3. Deposit & Withdrawal Flow

A clear "Add Money" and "Withdraw" flow with bank account linking, processing status, and estimated settlement times. Right now you have Paystack for payments, but the full deposit-to-investment journey could be more polished.
4. Watchlist

You have the data model for it (mentioned in useProfile), but a dedicated Watchlist tab or section where users can track stocks they're interested in before buying — with price alerts.
5. Dividend Tracking

Real investment apps show dividend income — when dividends were paid, how much, and total dividend yield. This adds real value for users holding JSE stocks.
6. Tax Reports

South African investors need IT3(b) and CGT info. Even a simple annual tax summary showing realized gains, dividends received, and fees paid would be a big differentiator.
7. Security & Session Management

Session timeout after inactivity (like banking apps)
"Active Sessions" view so users can see where they're logged in
PIN/passcode as a lighter security layer for quick access
8. Push Notifications

Your notification system is in-app only. Real fintech apps send push notifications for price movements, transaction confirmations, and KYC updates.
9. Onboarding Tutorial

A first-time user walkthrough that highlights key features — "Here's your portfolio," "Tap here to invest," etc. Helps with retention.
10. Performance Benchmarking

Show how the user's portfolio performs vs. a benchmark like the JSE All Share Index. "Your portfolio is up 8% vs. JSE Top 40 at 5%."
