                <p style="margin:0 0 16px;">Dear Mint Circle,</p>

                <p style="margin:0 0 20px;">We know many of you are constantly on the move. To keep you informed without adding noise, we have put together a concise summary of the key measures announced in the 2026 Budget Speech on 25 February 2026.</p>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">1) VAT Registration Threshold</p>
                <p style="margin:0 0 20px;">The compulsory VAT registration threshold increases from <strong>R1,000,000</strong> to <strong>R2,300,000</strong> in annual turnover.</p>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">2) Fuel Levy Adjustment</p>
                <ul style="margin:0 0 20px;padding-left:20px;">
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Total fuel levy increases by <strong>21 cents per litre</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">General fuel levy rises by <strong>9c for petrol</strong> and <strong>8c for diesel</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">RAF levy increases by <strong>7c</strong>.</li>
                </ul>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">3) Alcohol and Tobacco Excise</p>
                <ul style="margin:0 0 20px;padding-left:20px;">
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Cigarettes (20 pack): <strong>R22.81</strong> to <strong>R23.58</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Beer or cider (340ml): <strong>+8c</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Wine (750ml): <strong>+15c</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Spirits (750ml): <strong>+R3.20</strong>.</li>
                </ul>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">4) Tax-Free Savings and Retirement Incentives</p>
                <ul style="margin:0 0 20px;padding-left:20px;">
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Annual TFSA contribution limit increases from <strong>R36,000</strong> to <strong>R46,000</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Retirement fund deduction limit increases from <strong>R350,000</strong> to <strong>R430,000</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Primary residence CGT exclusion increases from <strong>R2,000,000</strong> to <strong>R3,000,000</strong>.</li>
                </ul>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">5) Social Grants and Consumer Spend</p>
                <ul style="margin:0 0 20px;padding-left:20px;">
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Old age grant increases from <strong>R2,315</strong> to <strong>R2,400</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Child support grant increases from <strong>R560</strong> to <strong>R580</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Foster care grant increases from <strong>R1,250</strong> to <strong>R1,295</strong>.</li>
                </ul>

                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;font-weight:700;color:#0f172a;">6) Personal Income Tax Adjustments</p>
                <p style="margin:0 0 8px;">Tax brackets are adjusted for inflation:</p>
                <ul style="margin:0 0 20px;padding-left:20px;">
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">First bracket moves from <strong>R237,100</strong> to <strong>R245,100</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Top bracket now starts at <strong>R1,878,600</strong> (previously <strong>R1,817,000</strong>).</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Primary rebate increases from <strong>R17,235</strong> to <strong>R17,820</strong>.</li>
                  <li style="margin-bottom:6px;">Tax-free threshold (under 65) rises from <strong>R95,750</strong> to <strong>R99,000</strong>.</li>
                </ul>

                <p style="margin:0 0 20px;">As always, we remain focused on translating policy developments into practical financial implications for our managed portfolios. Accordingly, our platform strategies continue to be actively managed within their stated mandates to reflect prevailing economic, fiscal and market conditions.</p>

                <p style="margin:0;font-weight:600;color:#0f172a;">Warm regards,<br/>The Mint Team</p>
